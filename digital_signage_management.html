Project Description

It is very common to see digital signages at eateries, universities, company lounges, hospitals and more. There are a lot of different free/paid solutions that will organize the contents of what will be displayed through customers' screens. However, for small business owners and organizations who don't have enough ability to hire professional web developer, it is very difficult to use most of the existing solutions. Thus, I decided to build up an open source CMS(Content Management System) that will easily guide people to make amazing and useful digital signage with minimal effort.

Technical Details

I will be utilizing following technical specifications:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • BootStrap
  • Drupal

Project Timeframe

This project has officially launched in December 2017. I'm expecting a minimum of 1 year to get the system fully functional.
