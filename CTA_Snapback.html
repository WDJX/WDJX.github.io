Project Description

When I was in high school and taking a graphic design course, one of the assignments was to make a new design for one of the potential school fashion line, snapback. I just did my best to utilize the school symbol which was wolf, and somehow my arts teacher loved it. My classmates loved it, even too much. It has been selected and it is still used for production for school apparel line.
